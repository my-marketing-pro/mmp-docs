## üéØ Our Approach

We balance **client satisfaction** with **product vision**, prioritize **speed with quality**, and believe in **transparent communication** throughout the development process.

Our product management philosophy:
- **Client-first** (transitioning to product-led)
- **Data-driven prioritization** (RICE scoring)
- **Quality releases** (3-stage review process)
- **Living documentation** (updated as we learn)

## üë• Team Roles

- **Product Manager**: Prioritization, roadmap, stakeholder communication
- **Founder**: Final decision maker, strategic direction
- **Account Manager**: Client liaison, feature requests from clients
- **Development Team**: Implementation, technical feasibility
- **Sales Team**: Revenue-impacting feature requests

## üöÄ Quick Start

**New to product management?** Start here:

1. Read the [SOP Overview](/mmp-docs/product-management/sop-overview/) to understand our philosophy
2. Review the [Feature Request Process](/mmp-docs/product-management/feature-request-process/) for day-to-day work
3. Familiarize yourself with [Frameworks & Tools](/mmp-docs/product-management/frameworks-and-tools/)
4. Check the [Release Process](/mmp-docs/product-management/release-process/) before shipping features

**Handling a feature request?**
1. Log it in Linear with the `feature-request` label
2. Triage within 48 hours (see [Feature Request Process](/mmp-docs/product-management/feature-request-process/))
3. Score with RICE framework
4. Communicate decision back to requester

**Releasing a feature?**
1. Schedule founder review (1:1 demo)
2. QA with team members
3. Deploy to production with release notes
4. Monitor for 24 hours
